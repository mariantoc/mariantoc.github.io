The Andes are home to 95% of the world's tropical glaciers. Since the 70s, glaciers throughout the region have retreated rapidly. The Humboldt peak in the Venezuelan Andes is one of the five main tropical glaciers in the country; It is located in the Sierra Nevada de Mérida and due to climate change it is at risk of disappearing. If urgent measures are not taken, it is expected to melt completely in one to two decades…
