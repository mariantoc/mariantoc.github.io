Espeleología / Speleology:

Venezuela cuenta con una gran patrimonio espeleológico, con alrededor de 650 cavidades catastradas a nivel nacional, algunas de ellas de una gran riqueza biológica, geológica y antropológica

Venezuela has a great speleological heritage, with around 650 caves registered nationwide, some of them of a great biological, geological and anthropological wealth.

Sveite, a new mineral from Autana Cave, Territorio Federal Amazonas, Venezuela.

April 2, 2022 Speleology

Descripcion: An unidentified mineral collected in a cave at Cerro Autana by the members of the "Sociedad Venezolana de Espeleologia" had been described by Franco Urbani in 1977. Both the mineral and the name have been approved by the I.M.A. Commission on New Minerals and Mineral Names. The name is derived after Sociedad Venezolana de Espeleogia. Type material is deposited at the Mineralogy Section of the School of Geology, Universidad Central de Venezuela, Caracas and at the Departamento de Espeleologia Ffsica, Sociedad Venezolana de Espeleologia, Caracas.
